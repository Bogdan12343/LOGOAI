Help Center

Find answers to the most common questions about LOGOAI.

Getting Started

How do I create my first logo?
Simply enter your company name, select your industry and preferred style, then click Generate. Your AI logo will be ready in seconds.
Do I need design experience?
No design experience needed. LOGOAI is built for everyone — founders, creators, and businesses of all sizes.
How many logos can I generate for free?
You get 1 free logo generation when you create an account. Upgrade to Pro for 50 generations per month.

Plans & Billing

How do I upgrade to Pro?
Click the "Upgrade Now" button in your dashboard. You'll be redirected to our secure payment page.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel anytime from your dashboard. You'll retain Pro access until the end of your billing period.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes! We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. Contact support@logoai.com for refund requests.

Downloads & Files

What file formats are available?
Free plan includes PNG. Pro and Agency plans include PNG, SVG, and PDF exports.
Can I use my logo commercially?
Yes! Pro and Agency plans include full commercial licensing rights.
